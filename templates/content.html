What can I find here?

Hello readers. First of all, I have to say that my English isn't perfect, so I am sorry if you read any misspell or grammatical error. Nonetheless, I will try to decrease them with all my strength.

With that already said, I will explain to you what you can find here. My blog posts consist in the use of code or other tools to analyze something. It can be anything, but since I am a geologist you will find most of all, spatial analysis, chilean geology, and GNSS time series data analysis.

You can use the right bar to navigate through my posts.

I use (most of all) my own tools for the blog posts. So if you want to check the code or use it yourself, I have a section with a list of my own projects. Right now my only project is GNSS Field Analysis (GFA), but when the blog gets bigger and GFA becomes more usable, I will start other projects.

Finally, in some posts I also share the data (if it is public), which I used in them.You can download them in the Data section.
