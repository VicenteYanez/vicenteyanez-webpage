About me

My name is Vicente and I am from Chile. I have a bachelor's degree of Geology in Universidad de Concepción the year 2016 and a Master Geophysical degree in Universidad de Chile. I am more interested in geospatial, geophysical and geological data analysis than in classical geology (mapping, field job and stuff like that).

If you have a project where I can participate, these are some of the thing that I can do: gather and clean geospatial data, modelate (classical way or machine learning), visualize and interpret them. Also, even though I am not a professional programmer I’m very good with python so if you need to solve a problem with code, I am your man.

Also, you could contact me and invite me some beers if you are in Concepción, Chile ;)
